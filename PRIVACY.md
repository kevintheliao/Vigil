# Vigil Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 11, 2026**

Vigil is an on-device SMS safety app that helps you identify scam and phishing text messages. This policy explains what Vigil does — and, more importantly, what it does not do — with your data.

## The short version

Vigil processes everything on your device. It has no internet access, no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Your messages never leave your phone.

## Data we collect

**None.** Vigil does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

## Permissions Vigil uses and why

### SMS access (`READ_SMS`)

Vigil reads incoming text messages solely to analyze them for signs of scams and phishing. This analysis happens entirely on your device using local detection logic.

- Message content is never transmitted anywhere.
- Message content is never stored by Vigil beyond the moment of analysis.
- No human ever sees your messages.

### Display over other apps (`SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW`)

Vigil uses this permission to show a warning overlay when a suspicious message is detected, so you can see the alert no matter which app you are using. The overlay is generated locally and involves no data collection.

## Network access

Vigil does not request the internet permission. The app is technically incapable of sending data off your device.

## Accounts

Vigil has no sign-up, no login, and no user accounts. We cannot identify you because we have no way to know who you are.

## Data storage

The only data Vigil stores is a small local settings file on your device (for example, whether you have completed the app's introduction). This never leaves your phone, and it is deleted when you uninstall the app. If you have Android device backup enabled, this settings file may be included in your device backup, which is managed by Android and your device manufacturer, not by Vigil.

## Third parties

Vigil uses no third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services. No data is shared with anyone, because no data is collected.

## Children's privacy

Vigil does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Data deletion

There is nothing to delete on our end — Vigil holds no data about you. To remove the app's local settings, uninstall the app.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new "Last updated" date. Because Vigil collects no data, any future change that introduced data collection would be accompanied by a clear in-app notice.
